Privacy Policy

Smart QR Pocket

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

Introduction

One2022 ("we", "our" or "company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store and protect your information when you use the Smart QR Pocket application ("app", "service").

By using our app, you agree to the information collection and use practices described in this privacy policy.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Types of Information We Collect

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage statistics, feature usage frequency, crash reports
  • Camera Data: Used only for scanning QR codes and barcodes, we do not store scan content
  • Storage Permissions: Used to save scan history and user settings (only on local device)

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, address, phone number)
  • Scanned QR code or barcode content
  • Camera-captured images or videos
  • Location information
  • Contact information

2. Information Usage

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Provide and improve app functionality
  • Analyze app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Provide technical support
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Permissions Explanation

3.1 Camera Permission

Used for scanning QR codes and barcodes. We do not store, upload, or share any images or video content obtained through the camera.

3.2 Storage Permission

Used to locally store scan history and app settings on your device. This data is only stored on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

3.3 Network Permission

Used only for displaying relevant ads and checking for app updates. We do not transmit your personal data or scan content over the network.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We promise:

  • We DO NOT sell your personal information
  • We DO NOT rent your personal information to third parties
  • We DO NOT share your scan data or personal content

4.1 Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying ads, following Google advertising policies
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics (if enabled)

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Principle of least privilege
  • Regular security reviews
  • Timely security vulnerability fixes

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for necessary periods:

  • Usage statistics: Maximum 12 months
  • Crash reports: Maximum 6 months
  • Local storage data: User controlled, can be deleted anytime

7. User Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Know what information we collect about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain your data in a structured format

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child, we will immediately delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in locations outside your country/region. We will ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.
